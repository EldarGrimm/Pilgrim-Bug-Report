How to Write a Helpful Bug Report

Tips for reporting bugs:

  • Describe what you expected to happen vs what actually happened.
  • Mention the location/level/scene where it occurred.
  • Include steps to reproduce the bug.
  • List your platform (Windows/Mac/etc.), controller, or settings if relevant.
  • Attach screenshots or videos if possible (not supported yet on this form).
