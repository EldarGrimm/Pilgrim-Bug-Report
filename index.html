How to Write a Helpful Bug Report

Tips for reporting bugs:

  • When do I report?: Whenever something goes wrong, feels off, or could be improved.
  • Mention the location/level/scene where it occurred.
  • If you are reporting a bug, please try to include steps to reproduce the bug.
  • If you are reporting something that looks or feels strange, describe what it should be like.
  • If you are reporting something unfun, tell us why it isn't and how we could make it better.
